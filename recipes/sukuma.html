SUKUMA WIKI

Description:

Collard greens from Kenya

Ingredients:

  • 1 bunch of kayle
  • 2 tbl spoons of cooking oil
  • 1 large onion
  • 2 tomatoes

Steps:

  1. Wash the kale thoroughly remove the thick stems and slice the leaves into thin uniform strips.
  2. Heat oil in a large pan over medium high heat.
  3. Add the diced onions and cook until soft and slightly golden.
  4. Add diced tomatoes and cook until they are soft and turn to paste.
  5. Add the sliced kayle to the pan with the tomato-onion mixture.
  6. Cook for 5 minutes stiring frequently until the leaves turn tender but are still bright green.
