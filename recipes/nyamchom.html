NYAMA CHOMA

Description:

Grilled meat served alongside ugali and sukuma wiki from Kenya

Ingredients:

  • 2 kg of beef
  • 1 tbsp ginger and garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp of soy sauce

Steps:

  1. Marinade the beef with the ginger, garlic and soy sauce.
  2. Let the meat rest for 1 hour.
  3. Grill over medium heat untile tender turning regularly to avoid burning.
  4. Let the meat rest for 10 minutes after cooking before serving to retain juices.
