UGALI

Description:

Ugali is a simple, stiff porridge staple from Kenya

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of water.
  • 1 1/2 cups of maize flour.

Steps:

  1. Boil 2 cups of water in a sufuria.
  2. Gradually stir in 1 1/2 cups of maize flour.
  3. Cook on low heat for 15 minutes.
  4. Continously stir and press against the pot to break lumps.
  5. It is ready once it forms a smooth thick dough like consistency and separates from the sufuria.
